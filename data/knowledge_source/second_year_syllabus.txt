### DETAILED SYLLABUS (CSE CORE)

## Analysis and Design of Algorithms (DSC-7)
**Unit 1:** Fundamentals: Time/Space Complexity, Asymptotic Notations (Big O, Theta, Omega).
**Unit 2:** Divide & Conquer: Merge Sort, Quick Sort, Master Theorem, Matrix Multiplication.
**Unit 3:** Dynamic Programming & Greedy: Fibonacci, LCS, Knapsack, Activity Selection, Huffman Coding, Prim's/Kruskal's.
**Unit 4:** Graphs & Advanced: BFS, DFS, Dijkstra, Bellman-Ford, Ford-Fulkerson, NP-Completeness .

## Digital System Design (DSC-8)
**Unit 1:** Logic Fundamentals: Boolean Algebra, K-Maps, Logic Gates, Multiplexers, Decoders.
**Unit 2:** Sequential Logic: Flip-Flops (SR, D, JK, T), State Diagrams, Counters, Shift Registers.
**Unit 3:** HDLs: VHDL/Verilog syntax, modeling combinational/sequential circuits.
**Unit 4:** Advanced Design: Arithmetic Circuits (Adders, ALUs), FSM design, Datapath/Control path.

## Database Management Systems (DSC-9)
**Unit 1:** Intro: Architecture, Data Models, Data Independence.
**Unit 2:** Modeling: ER Diagrams, Normalization (1NF to 5NF).
**Unit 3:** SQL: DDL, DML, Joins, Subqueries, Transactions (ACID), Concurrency Control.
**Unit 4:** Advanced: NoSQL (Key-Value, Document), Big Data basics, Data Warehousing [cite: 4209-4216].

## Operating System (DSC-10)
**Unit 1:** Intro: System calls, Kernel structures (Monolithic/Micro).
**Unit 2:** Process Mgmt: Scheduling, IPC, Threads, Deadlocks (Prevention/Avoidance), Semaphores.
**Unit 3:** Memory: Paging, Segmentation, Virtual Memory, Page Replacement.
**Unit 4:** Storage & Security: File Systems, Disk Scheduling, Authentication, Access Control [cite: 4400-4408].

## Software Engineering (DSC-11)
**Unit 1:** SDLC Models: Waterfall, Agile, Spiral, DevOps. Requirements Engineering.
**Unit 2:** Design: UML (Use Case, Class, Sequence), Design Patterns (MVC, Singleton).
**Unit 3:** Testing: Unit/Integration/System testing, Black-box vs White-box.
**Unit 4:** Project Mgmt: Cost Estimation (COCOMO), Risk Mgmt, Version Control [cite: 4434-4441].

## Computer System Architecture (DSC-12)
**Unit 1:** Basics: Von Neumann, RISC vs CISC, Computer Arithmetic.
**Unit 2:** Processor: ALU, Control Unit, Pipelining, Superscalar, Cache Mapping.
**Unit 3:** Memory: RAM types, ROM, RAID, Virtual Memory (TLB).
**Unit 4:** I/O: DMA, Bus protocols, Interrupts [cite: 4474-4482].

### DETAILED SYLLABUS (ECE CORE)

## Electronic Devices and Circuits (DSC-7)
**Unit 1:** Diodes: P-N Junction, Zener, LED, Rectifiers, Filters.
**Unit 2:** Transistors: BJT (CB, CE, CC), FET/MOSFET construction & characteristics.
**Unit 3:** Biasing: Stability factors, Thermal runaway, Heat sinks.
**Unit 4:** Amplifiers: h-parameters, Small signal models, High frequency analysis.

## Network Analysis and Synthesis (DSC-8)
**Unit 1:** Basics: Thevenin, Norton, Superposition, Max Power Transfer.
**Unit 2:** Graph Theory: Incidence matrix, Cut-sets, KVL/KCL.
**Unit 3:** Transient Analysis: Laplace transforms, Poles/Zeros, Impulse/Step response.
**Unit 4:** Two-Port Networks: Z, Y, ABCD, h-parameters, Network Synthesis (RC, RL, LC).

## Digital Electronics - I (DSC-9)
**Unit 1:** Boolean Algebra: K-Maps, Logic Families (TTL, CMOS).
**Unit 2:** Combinational Logic: Adders, Subtractors, Encoders/Decoders, MUX/DEMUX.
**Unit 3:** Sequential Logic: Flip-Flops, Counters (Ripple, Synchronous), Shift Registers.
**Unit 4:** Converters: ADC (Flash, Successive Approx), DAC (R-2R), Memories (ROM, RAM).

## Signals and Systems (DSC-10)
**Unit 1:** Basics: Continuous/Discrete signals, LTI Systems, Convolution.
**Unit 2:** Fourier Analysis: Fourier Series, CTFT, DTFT, Frequency response.
**Unit 3:** Laplace & Z-Transform: ROC, Inverse transforms, Pole-Zero plots.
**Unit 4:** Sampling: Sampling theorem, Aliasing, Reconstruction.

## Electromagnetic Theory (DSC-11)
**Unit 1:** Electrostatics: Gauss Law, Poisson/Laplace equations, Magnetostatics (Biot-Savart).
**Unit 2:** Maxwell’s Equations: Faraday’s Law, Boundary Conditions, Wave Equation, Poynting Vector.
**Unit 3:** Transmission Lines: Impedance, VSWR, Smith Chart.
**Unit 4:** Waveguides & Antennas: Rectangular waveguides, TE/TM modes, Dipole antenna basics.

## Linear Integrated Circuits (DSC-12)
**Unit 1:** Op-Amps: Ideal vs Real, Slew Rate, CMRR, Current Mirrors.
**Unit 2:** Applications: Inverting/Non-inverting, Integrator, Differentiator, Schmitt Trigger.
**Unit 3:** Filters & Oscillators: Active filters (Butterworth), RC Phase Shift, Wien Bridge.
**Unit 4:** PLL & Timers: 555 Timer (Astable/Monostable), PLL (Phase Locked Loop), ADC/DAC .

---
### ECE ELECTIVES (DSE)
**Computational Methods:** Roots of equations, Numerical Integration, Interpolation (Newton/Lagrange).
**PCB Design:** SMD, Layout rules, EMI/EMC compliance, Etching, Soldering.
**Interfacing Electronics:** Arduino/Raspberry Pi, GPIO, I2C, SPI, Sensors, Actuators.
**Modeling Electronic Circuits:** SPICE simulation, Device models (BJT/MOSFET), Transient analysis.

### DETAILED SYLLABUS (EE CORE)

## Electrical Network Analysis (DSC-7)
**Unit 1:** AC Circuits: Resonance, Magnetically coupled circuits, Dot convention.
**Unit 2:** Theorems: Thevenin, Norton, Superposition, Millman, Tellegen. Graph Theory.
**Unit 3:** Transients: Laplace transforms, Initial/Final value theorems, RLC transients.
**Unit 4:** Two-Port Networks: Z, Y, ABCD, Hybrid parameters,Filter synthesis .

## Electrical Machines-I (DSC-8)
**Unit 1:** Energy Conversion: Magnetic circuits, Singly/Doubly excited systems, Torque production.
**Unit 2:** DC Generators: Lap/Wave winding, EMF equation, Excitation methods.
**Unit 3:** DC Motors: Torque equation, Speed control, Braking, Swinburne’s test.
**Unit 4:** Transformers: Single/Three-phase, Phasor diagrams, Efficiency, Auto-transformers.

## Analog and Digital Electronic Circuits (DSC-9)
**Unit 1:** Op-Amps: Slew rate, Offset, Integrator, Differentiator, Instrumentation Amp.
**Unit 2:** Special Circuits: Precision Rectifiers, Peak Detectors, 555 Timer, PLL.
**Unit 3:** Combinational Logic: K-Maps, MUX/DEMUX, Adders, Encoders.
**Unit 4:** Sequential Logic: Flip-Flops, Counters, Shift Registers, ADC/DAC .

## Electrical Machines-II (DSC-10)
**Unit 1:** Induction Motors: 3-Phase construction, Torque-slip, Circle diagram, Speed control (V/f).
**Unit 2:** Alternators: EMF equation, Armature reaction, Voltage regulation, Parallel operation.
**Unit 3:** Synchronous Motors: V-Curves, Hunting, Starting methods.
**Unit 4:** Single Phase Motors: Split phase, Capacitor start/run, Stepper, BLDC.

## Power Transmission and Distribution (DSC-11)
**Unit 1:** Structure: Substations, Skin/Proximity effect, L/C calculation.
**Unit 2:** Performance: Short/Medium/Long lines, Ferranti effect, Corona.
**Unit 3:** Mechanical Design: Sag/Tension, Insulators (String efficiency), Underground cables.
**Unit 4:** Protection: Surges, Lightning arrestors, Traveling waves .

## Electrical and Electronic Measurements (DSC-12)
**Unit 1:** Instruments: PMMC, Moving Iron, Dynamometer, Errors.
**Unit 2:** Power/Energy: Wattmeters, Energy meters, Potentiometers.
**Unit 3:** Bridges: Maxwell, Hay, Anderson, Schering bridges. Cable fault location.
**Unit 4:** Electronics: CRO, Spectrum Analyzer, Transducers (LVDT, Strain Gauge).

---
### EE ELECTIVES (DSE)
**Non-Conventional Energy:** Solar (PV/Thermal), Wind, Biomass, Geothermal, Ocean energy.
**Signal and Systems:** LTI systems, Fourier/Laplace/Z-transforms, Sampling theorem.
**Electrical Engineering Materials:** Conductors, Dielectrics, Magnetic materials, Semiconductors.
**Electrical Machine Design:** Transformer design (Core/Windings), DC Machine design, Induction Motor design.
